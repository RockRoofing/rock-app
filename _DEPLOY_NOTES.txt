DEPLOY NOTES - VAT: fix speed + Box 5 accuracy
==============================================

1) SPEED
--------
The page was recomputing the whole estimate from Xero (paging through all invoices, bills
and credit notes) on EVERY load - slow. Now:
  - Normal page load reads a CACHED estimate from Redis (instant).
  - A new button "Refresh estimate from Xero" recomputes + caches it (use this
    occasionally, or when you want the current-month estimate updated).
  - Entering a filed Box 5 no longer calls Xero at all - it just saves and re-reads the
    cache, so it's instant.
First time: click "Refresh estimate from Xero" once to populate the cache.

2) BOX 5 ACCURACY
-----------------
Bug: after entering a filed Box 5, the "Estimated net" column flipped its sign and looked
wrong, because that cell was mixing the filed figure into the estimate maths. Fixed - the
estimate columns (Output/Input VAT est, Estimated net) now ALWAYS show the raw transaction
estimate, completely independent of the filed Box 5. The "Position used" column is the one
that switches to your filed figure. So:
  - Output VAT (est) / Input VAT (est) / Estimated net = always the transaction estimate.
  - Filed Box 5 = what you type.
  - Position used = filed figure for past months (green "Filed"), estimate otherwise.
