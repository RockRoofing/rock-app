DEPLOY NOTES - Gross Margin now comes LIVE from Xero (last 6 completed months, rolling)
=======================================================================================

WHAT CHANGED
------------
The Commercial Scorecard's Gross Margin card no longer computes margin from project
lines (invoiced vs costs at valuation date). It now reads the company Gross Margin
straight from Xero's Profit & Loss:

    Gross Margin = (Sales - Direct Costs) / Sales      (OVERHEADS EXCLUDED)

  - Sales        = Xero P&L "Income" section total (account 200 etc.)
  - Direct Costs = Xero P&L "Cost of Sales / Direct Costs" section total
  - Overheads    = excluded by design.

  - Headline number = the LAST FULLY COMPLETED calendar month's Gross Margin.
  - Trend line      = the last 6 completed months, rolling (e.g. in Sept: Mar..Aug).
  - Click the card  = per-month breakdown table (Month, Sales, Direct Costs,
                      Gross Profit, Gross Margin), newest first.

Data is LIVE-FETCHED from Xero on every Scorecard load (about 6 Xero calls, one per
month). Nothing is stored in Redis for this - it's always current.

CREDIT NOTES
------------
No special handling needed and NONE was added on purpose. Xero's P&L is accrual-based
and already nets credit notes into the section totals:
  - Sales credit notes (ACCRECCREDIT) reduce the Income total.
  - Supplier credit notes (ACCPAYCREDIT) reduce the Cost of Sales total.
So using the P&L section totals accounts for credit notes automatically. Fetching the
/CreditNotes endpoint on top would DOUBLE COUNT, so we deliberately do not.
