--------------

  1. The sales ledger stored signs one way, and
  2. the sales API view then FLIPPED every amount again (a leftover from when the data
     came from the general-ledger endpoint).
Net result: everything was inverted - sales (credits) showed negative, reductions
(debits) showed positive. May stood out because its bar (from the P&L, +92k) didn't
match its inverted table total (-102k). The other months were inverted too; they just
weren't scrutinised.

-----------------------------------------------
- Removed the double sign-flip in the sales view.
- Manual journals: a sale is a Xero CREDIT -> now shows POSITIVE; a reversal is a DEBIT
  -> NEGATIVE.
- Invoices: sales POSITIVE; debit "Dis"/adjustment lines NEGATIVE.
- Credit notes: NEGATIVE (reductions).
