WHAT CHANGED
------------
1) SALES CREDIT NOTES: now pulls ACCREC credit notes (ACCRECCREDIT) coded to your sales
   codes and shows them as NEGATIVE (reductions), per your steer. Codes stay 200/260/270.
2) SOURCE COLUMN: the detail table now has a "Source" column showing whether each line
   is an Invoice, a Manual journal (WIP), or a Credit note. Manual journals are purple,
   credit notes red. This makes any "cost-looking" line identifiable at a glance.

ABOUT THE "COSTS" YOU SAW
-------------------------
The fetch only ever keeps lines coded to 200/260/270 - it never pulls cost codes. So a
"cost-looking" row is almost certainly a MANUAL JOURNAL debit posted to a sales code
(e.g. a WIP reversal), which correctly shows as a negative sale. The new Source column
lets you confirm this: sort by Amount or look at Source.

     Code + a couple of descriptions from those rows and I'll trace exactly where they
     come from. It's possible some non-sales activity is being posted to 260/270 - if so
     we may want to drop those codes and keep only 200 (quick change).
