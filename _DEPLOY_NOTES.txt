Scorecard — GP Margin card no longer overflows (target + indicator clipped)
===========================================================================
Deploy target: RockRoofing/rock-app  branch main
Build: Compiled successfully
Single file: pages/commercial-scorecard.js

ISSUE
-----
The scorecard cards had a FIXED height (210px). The Gross Profit Margin card has extra
content (Profit / Invoiced / Costs lines + a longer sub-heading), which pushed the
Target control and the RAG indicator (the coloured dot) below the fixed height — so
they sat outside the card and were hidden by the card below.

FIX
---
Cards now use minHeight instead of a fixed height, so a card grows to fit its content.
The Target row and indicator stay inside the GP Margin card; other cards are unchanged
(they keep the same minimum height).
