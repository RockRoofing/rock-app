BUG 1 — "Clear all" left old data behind (your March 2024 bills)
---------------------------------------------------------------
The clear only deleted data for CURRENT Xero projects (from getProjectsFromCategories).
Bills for ARCHIVED/old/completed projects are stored under tracking IDs that aren't
in the current list, so they survived the clear — which is why March 2024 (£126k on
28 Mar etc.) still showed after you "cleared all".

FIX: the clear now SCANS all matching Redis keys (costs:bills:*, costs:wages:*,
invoiced:lines:*, invoiced:latest:*) and deletes every one — archived projects
included. So "Clear" now truly removes everything of that type.
ACTION: run Data Management -> Clear Bills (type CLEAR) again after deploying to
remove the leftover old bills, then re-upload.

BUG 2 — "471 days not in this file" warning on every upload
-----------------------------------------------------------
The warning listed EVERY day in the app not in the file — so a 1-month upload
always flagged all ~3 years of other days. Correct logic, useless in practice.

FIX: the warning now only flags days WITHIN the file's own date span (between its
earliest and latest date) that the file skipped — i.e. genuine gaps in the period
you actually uploaded (e.g. file covers June but missed 14 June). Days outside the
file's period are no longer listed. This makes the warning meaningful and quiet.
