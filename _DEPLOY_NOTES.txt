THE PROBLEM YOU SPOTTED
-----------------------
Invoices in Xero that don't carry the Projects tracking category (many retention
invoices — e.g. Christchurch, Russell Hill, Glenvale Park) were being SILENTLY
DROPPED on import, because the app matches invoices to projects via that tag.
That's why Xero showed them but the app didn't. (Also note: one of the two CSVs
you uploaded — the 11_56_54 file — was effectively empty of outstanding balances;
use the full export, i.e. the 11_56_03-style one.)

THE FIX (your choice: show them, don't guess)
---------------------------------------------
• Invoices with NO Projects tag are now collected into a reserved "Unassigned"
  bucket instead of being dropped.
• They appear in Outstanding Invoices like any other invoice, with an amber
  "UNASSIGNED" badge and the project name "Unassigned (no project tag in Xero)".
• No guessing / auto-assignment — they're shown exactly as-is so nothing is hidden.
• On your real file this surfaces ~15 outstanding untagged invoices (and 297
  untagged invoices in total) that were previously invisible.

TO PROPERLY ATTRIBUTE THEM
--------------------------
Tag the invoice with the Projects tracking category in Xero, then re-upload — it
will move from "Unassigned" to its project automatically. (Uploads merge/dedupe,
so re-uploading is safe.)

• pages/api/import-invoices-bulk.js — untagged -> __UNASSIGNED__ bucket (not dropped)
• pages/api/dashboard.js            — exposes an "Unassigned" pseudo-project
