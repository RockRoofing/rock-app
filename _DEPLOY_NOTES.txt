BUG 1 — WIP tab empty despite EOM showing WIP
---------------------------------------------
The WIP tab's valuation-date dropdown (pastVDates) was built ONLY from the fixed
"valuation day of month" and returned nothing if no fixed day was set — so
projects with only MANUAL monthly valuation dates had no date to work from, hence
no post-valuation costs and no WIP. EOM worked because it already honoured the
manual overrides.
Fix: getPastValuationDates now MERGES the fixed-day dates AND the manual
dateOverrides valuation dates (overrides win for their month), and runs even when
there's no fixed day. The WIP tab now shows costs/WIP for override-only projects,
matching EOM.

BUG 2 — double minus on the WIP Value card
------------------------------------------
"costs / (1 - -12.5%)" appeared when margin was negative. Reworded to
"costs ÷ (1 − margin), margin −12.5%".
