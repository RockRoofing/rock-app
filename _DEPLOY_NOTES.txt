DEPLOY NOTES - Overhead Budgets: gear row-hide, single Budget column, clear-override
====================================================================================

CHANGES
-------
1) GEAR ICON (top-right of the table) -> "Show / hide accounts".
   - Opens a checklist of every overhead account; untick to hide that whole ROW.
   - "Show all" / "Hide all" shortcuts. A "hidden" count shows next to the gear.
   - The selection is SHARED and PERSISTED for everyone (stored in Redis:
     config:overhead-hidden-rows). "Done & save" writes it; the main Save also
     persists it.

2) BUDGET is now a single COLUMN (one flat monthly figure per account), placed
   immediately to the right of the Account name. The old per-cell budget boxes are
   gone. Every completed month is coloured against this one budget:
   green at/under budget, red over.
   (Storage config:overhead-budgets changed from { code: { month: amt } } to a flat
    { code: amt }. Any previously-saved per-month budgets are ignored; just re-enter
    the flat monthly budget once per account.)

3) CLEAR BUTTON on overridden future cells. Type a value into a future (forecast)
   cell to override it; a small red x appears - click it to clear the override and
   revert the cell to its automatic forecast.

Unchanged: forecast method selector per line (1 avg this FY / 2 last 3 mo / 3 same
mo last yr), FY switcher (Dec-Nov), actuals from the P&L benchmark.
