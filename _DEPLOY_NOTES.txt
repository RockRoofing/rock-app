1) OVERDUE-BY NOW MATCHES XERO (off-by-one fixed)
-------------------------------------------------
Xero counts the overdue period INCLUSIVELY (the day after the due date is "1 day
overdue"). The app was showing one day fewer. Now adds 1 once past the due date,
so "Overdue by" matches Xero exactly. (Verified against INV-1683: app was 19,
Xero 20 — now aligned.)

2) UNASSIGNED KEY ON OUTSTANDING INVOICES PAGE
----------------------------------------------
Added an amber "UNASSIGNED" key/legend strip below the summary cards, shown only
when there are untagged invoices: e.g. "15 invoices (£84,919.55) have no project
tracking category in Xero… tag them in Xero and re-upload." Explains the badge on
the rows.

3) UPLOAD NOW REPORTS NEW vs UPDATED (invoices)
-----------------------------------------------
The Sales Invoices upload result now shows "New added" and "Updated (already had)"
instead of just a total — so re-uploading the same file clearly shows 0 new / all
updated, confirming no duplicates were created. (The dedupe by invoice number was
already preventing duplicates; this just makes it visible.)
