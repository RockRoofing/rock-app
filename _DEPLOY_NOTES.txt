CHANGE
------
The pop-out previously saved silently on every tick/change, so it wasn't clear
anything had happened. Now:
  • Edits (day, hour, portal-user ticks, manual emails) update the form only.
  • A clear "Save" button persists everything at once and CLOSES the pop-out —
    so it's obvious it worked.
  • "Cancel" closes without saving those edits.
  • "Send now" still works, and now saves the on-screen recipients first so it
    sends to exactly who's shown (not a stale list).
