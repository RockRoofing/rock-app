This is the first of several bundles working through your 11-item list. It's
self-contained and safe to deploy/test on its own.

1) CRs AUTO-POPULATE THE EDIT-PROJECT-DETAILS BUDGETS (on LOCK)
   When a project's Contracted Rates are LOCKED (either via the Lock button or by
   saving while locked), the CR totals become the source of truth and OVERWRITE the
   edit-project-details fields:
     • Original Contract Value  <- CR "Above the line" total
     • Labour budget            <- CR "Labour budget (above)"
     • Materials budget         <- CR "Materials budget (above)"
   This also satisfies "uploading/relocking CRs overwrites manual entries" — each
   lock re-pushes the current CR totals. (Budgets only change on LOCK, so unlocked
   editing won't disturb the project details until you re-lock.)

2) SEARCHABLE PROJECT DROPDOWN ON THE CRs TAB
   The project selector is now a type-to-search box (filter by job no or name),
   replacing the long plain dropdown. (Applications tab gets the same in the next
   bundle, Group B.)
