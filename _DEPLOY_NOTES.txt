PLANNING — Gantt rework (selection + day/week view + inline dates + frozen column) & Operatives mandatory fields

pages/operations/project-management/planning.js
  1) INLINE DATE EDITING: Start and Contracted Completion are now date inputs directly in each project
     row — edit in place, saves on change. No dates in the allocate pop-up anymore.
  2) COLUMN DATES SHOW THE YEAR (W/C dd Mmm yy).
  3) DAY / WEEK VIEW TOGGLE (replaces the weeks dropdown). Default horizon = 3 years forward
     (Historic toggle adds 1 year back). WEEK VIEW is read-only: each column = a week; part-weeks
     fill proportionally by working days Mon–Fri (e.g. 3/5). Allocation is Day-view only.
  4) CELL SELECTION + DRAG: in Day view, click a day to select/deselect; drag across days to select a
     range (weekends can't be selected). A yellow action bar shows the count with an "Allocate labour →"
     button. Nothing opens on a plain click now — you choose the cells first.
  5) ALLOCATE POP-UP: no start/completion dates; instead lists the actual dates being allocated. Pick
     installers to apply across ALL selected days (full day). Clashes (installer already on another
     project that day) are skipped and reported. 
  6) ADD NEW OPERATIVE inline from the allocate pop-up (writes to the roster, then auto-selects them).
  7) FROZEN PROJECT + DATE COLUMNS: the Project / Start / Contracted Completion columns stay put when
     scrolling the calendar horizontally (sticky).

pages/operations/hs/operatives.js
  - Email, Phone, Company and at least one Trade are now MANDATORY (marked *), enforced on save.
