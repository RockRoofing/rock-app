DEPLOY NOTES - VAT Refund: manual filed Box 5 (past) + estimate (current/future)
================================================================================

WHAT BOX 5 IS
-------------
Box 5 is the headline net VAT figure on a UK VAT return: VAT on sales (Box 1) minus VAT
reclaimed on purchases (Box 4). If Box 4 is bigger (your usual case), Box 5 is a REFUND
from HMRC. Example: your June return had Box 1 6,535.40, Box 4 23,829.34, so Box 5 =
17,293.94 refund.

APPROACH (Option B - chosen)
----------------------------
We can't pull the filed return automatically (Xero won't grant your app the tax-reports
scope - it throws invalid_scope). So:
  - PAST months: you type the filed Box 5 straight off your Xero VAT return, and pick
    "refund" or "to pay". Saved permanently (Redis key vat:filed). This is the real,
    reliable figure.
  - CURRENT + FUTURE months: a transaction ESTIMATE (no return exists yet) - indicative
    for cash flow, moves as invoices/bills post.

connect.js is reverted to a working scope set (no tax-reports scope), so reconnect works.
You do NOT need to reconnect for this to function - it uses existing access.

HOW TO USE
----------
1. Open VAT Refund. Past months show a "Filed Box 5" input.
2. For each past month, type the Box 5 amount from your Xero VAT return and choose
   refund / to pay. It saves on tab-out (amount) or immediately (dropdown).
3. That month's "Position used" then shows your filed figure (green Filed); the chart
   updates. Leave a month blank to fall back to the estimate.
