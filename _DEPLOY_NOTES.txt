RAMS MATRIX
  - Projects down the side (auto from active Ops projects, sorted by number).
  - Installers across the top (auto from the Operatives roster, alphabetical; no Rock/subbie split).
  - Click a cell to toggle "Yes" = that installer has signed onto that project's RAMS.
  - Filter by Project / Operative / Company. Project column frozen when scrolling.
  - Store: ops:rams-matrix = { [projectNo]: { [opId]: true } }.

H&S TRAINING MATRIX
  - People down the side (alphabetical): Operatives roster + Portal users, de-duped by name. Blank cell
    when no record.
  - Columns: Employee (frozen), Company, Trade, Phone, Email, then the training columns.
  - Cells: click to set an EXPIRY DATE or tick "Does not expire". Colour: >2 months green, <2 months
    amber, past today red, blank if empty.
  - Add training columns (+ Add training column) and delete any column (× on the column header).
    Defaults seeded from the company's live tracker (33 trainings) on first load; fully editable after.
  - Filter by Person / Company / Trade. Store: ops:hs-matrix-columns + ops:hs-matrix-data.

MONTHLY EXPIRY EMAIL
  - pages/api/cron/hs-expiry-email.js emails all Operations Managers (team members whose role contains
    "Operations Manager"; falls back to ALERT_EMAIL) a digest of trainings expired + expiring within 2
    months. Runs DAILY but only actually sends on the 1st of the month. Manual test: hit
    /api/cron/hs-expiry-email?force=1 .
