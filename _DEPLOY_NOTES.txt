1. SITE CONTACTS TYPING BUG  (pages/operations/projects.js)
   ROOT CAUSE: the Section/L wrapper components were defined INSIDE ProjectDetails, so every keystroke

   Fixed by hoisting Section and L to module scope (stable identity). Role/Name/Email/Phone now type
   normally.

   Live Tasks warning).

   Added a DateColourKey legend (red = overdue/today or past, orange = due within a week, green = more than
   a week away) at the top of both tables, matching the existing dateCellStyle colours.

5. ISSUES PAGE — default to OPEN  (pages/operations/project-management/issues.js)
   The Status filter now defaults to "Open" when you land on the page.

   Replaced the bare "actual delivery date" field with a big "✓ Mark as delivered" button that auto-fills
   today's date (with an "Undo" and an editable date if it needs changing). No more manually entering a
   date to record a delivery.
