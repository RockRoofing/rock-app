Deploy target: RockRoofing/rock-app  branch main
Build: ✓ Compiled successfully  ·  tab shows "· v7"

CHANGE
------
Each line's ⋯ menu now has a "Format line" section with three toggles:
  • Bold
  • Underline
  • Red
Any combination can be applied, and they can be toggled on/off (a ✓ shows when
active). The menu stays open while you tick formats so you can apply several at
once. Works on both item rows and heading rows, and is independent of the
existing strike-through and the heading bold/underline styling.
Formatting is saved with the rates (persists on save/reload).
