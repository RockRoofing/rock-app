GANTT — Historic view scroll-back behaviour

pages/operations/project-management/planning.js

  - Clicking "Historic" now sets the view START POINT to 2 WEEKS BEFORE TODAY (previously it just widened
    the start slightly while staying anchored on today).
  - From there you can SCROLL BACK with the ‹ button, ONE WEEK at a time, down to a floor of 6 WEEKS before
    today — or FURTHER if there are allocations older than that (the floor extends to the earliest allocated
    week so you can always reach existing data).
  - The ‹ button disables at the back floor so you can't scroll into empty space.
  - In normal (non-historic) mode the ‹ / › buttons still jump 12 weeks; in Historic mode they move one
    week at a time for finer control. "Today" turns Historic off and snaps back to this week.
