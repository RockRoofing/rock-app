CHANGE
------
The tick-box column header now has a select-all checkbox. Ticking it selects
every item ABOVE and BELOW the line at once; unticking clears them. It shows an
indeterminate (–) state when only some rows are selected. (Section headings and
struck rows aren't selectable, so they're ignored.)
