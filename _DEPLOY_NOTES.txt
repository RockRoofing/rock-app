WHAT CHANGED
------------
Previously the Site Manager name/email were fields at the bottom of the CM's
sign screen — easy to miss, so approving errored with "please enter the
customer's Site Manager name".

Now the CM approval is a clear 3-step flow:
  1) Read the RAMS  (scroll to the bottom)
  2) ALLOCATE A SITE MANAGER  ← new dedicated screen
       • pick a project Site Contact, or enter name + email manually
       • both are mandatory; "Continue to sign" is blocked until valid
       • clearly states the Site Manager is emailed only AFTER the Director signs
  3) Sign  (draw signature → Approve & sign)
     - the sign screen shows a small confirmation of who was allocated

This only applies to the Contracts Manager flow. Operatives and the Director
still go straight Read → Sign.

FILE CHANGED
------------
• pages/forms/index.js  — new 'allocate-sm' step in the CM approval flow;
                          Site Manager fields moved out of the sign screen.

"Allocate a Site Manager" (choose contact or type name+email) → Continue to sign
→ draw signature → Approve & sign. Stage advances to Director; no email sent yet.
