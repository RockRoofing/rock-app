FIXES YOUR SALES ISSUE
----------------------
"Bookkeeper categorised sales in Xero but the app still shows them unassigned":
press ↻ Sync Invoices. It re-pulls sales for ALL projects from Xero over the
selected window (default 6 months) and exact-mirrors them, so newly-categorised
invoices move onto their project and drop off the unassigned list.

API SAFETY
----------
• Windowed (default 6 months) so pulls stay light.
• Sales/Wages are batched endpoints — a few calls per project; well under limits.
• 45-second cooldown per button (429 if pressed again too soon) to avoid
  accidental double-clicks tripping Xero's concurrency limit. No daily cap needed.
• Nightly global sync still refreshes sales+wages for all projects automatically;
  these buttons are the on-demand version.
