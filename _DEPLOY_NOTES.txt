WHY THEY DIDN'T MATCH
---------------------
Two different sign conventions:
  • Graph summed ABSOLUTE values (credit notes added).
  • Table "No category assigned" total sums SIGNED values (credit notes subtract).
So a month with any credits showed a higher figure on the graph than the table.

FIX
---
• The graph now sums SIGNED amounts, matching the table. Click a month on the
  graph and the table's "No category assigned" total for that month will equal the
  graph's point for that month.
• Y-axis now allows negatives (domain auto), in case a month nets negative.
• Caption clarified: the graph is the LAST 6 MONTHS. Note this is still a subset
  of the table when NO month is selected — the table (no month filter) also
  includes uncategorised items OUTSIDE the last 6 months and any with no parseable
  date. Selecting a month (or clicking the graph) makes them reconcile exactly.
