DELIVERIES APP — Scheduled / Delivered filter

  pages/forms/deliveries.js
   - After selecting a project, a two-way filter appears: "Scheduled" | "Delivered", defaulting to
     Scheduled.
       • Scheduled = deliveries not yet marked delivered (dated ones still limited to those due within the
         next 2 weeks / overdue, as before; undated ones always show).
       • Delivered = deliveries that have an actual delivery date recorded.
   - Empty-state text adapts to the selected filter.

Deploy this single file (supersedes the previous pages/forms/deliveries.js). No cron/env change.
