PDF (customer copy)
-------------------
Combined A4: Summary + Payment Certificate + Contract Works (measurable only) +
Variations (instructed only) + Materials on Site (marked-up Total, NO mark-up %
column), then variation + supplier-group attachments appended. Download via the
"Download PDF" button.

SEND TO CUSTOMER (popout)
-------------------------
• TO — exactly ONE recipient, chosen from a dropdown of Customer contacts OR Rock
  Roofing portal users.
• CC — any number, tickable from Customer contacts and Rock Roofing portal users,
  plus a free-text box for extra emails. (The chosen To can't also be CC'd.)
• MESSAGE — editable, pre-filled from this template (with a "Reset to template"
  link):
      Subject: Application for Payment [App No] - [Current Month] [Year]

      Hi [Customer first name],

      Please find attached our application for payment [App No] for [current month].

      Feel free to call if there is anything you would like to discuss.

      Kind Regards,

      [Your name]
      [Your email]
      [Your phone]
      Rock Roofing Ltd
  ([Your ...] = the signed-in portal user's name / email / phone.)
• Reply-to = the sender (signed-in portal user), falling back to the project QS.
• Optional "Mark as sent" (freezes variations + locks).

SENDER (from)
-------------
COMMERCIAL_FROM_EMAIL if set, else ACCOUNTS_FROM_EMAIL, else FORMS_FROM_EMAIL, else
Resend test address. Set COMMERCIAL_FROM_EMAIL in Vercel to
"Rock Roofing Commercial <commercial@rockroofing.co.uk>" (domain already verified
in Resend).
