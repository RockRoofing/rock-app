H&S MATRIX — locked columns = protect from deletion (not "required")

  pages/operations/hs/hs-matrix.js
   - Locking a column no longer stamps "required" on empty cells or outlines them red.
   - A locked column simply means its cells CAN'T BE CLEARED once they hold a value (you can still change
     the date). The Clear button is hidden for a locked cell that has a value, and clearing is blocked
     server-side-of-UI with a note. Empty locked cells behave normally.
   - Column menu checkbox relabelled "Lock column (cells can't be cleared)"; 🔒 shown on locked headers.

Deploy over previous hs-matrix.js. No API change.
