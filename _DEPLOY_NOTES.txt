Credit notes now included in invoiced / retention / EOM
=======================================================
Deploy target: RockRoofing/rock-app  branch main
Build: ✓ Compiled successfully
Single file: pages/api/sync-invoices.js

THE PROBLEM
-----------
Sales credit notes weren't reflected in Project Financials (invoiced), the Retention
tracker, or the EOM report. Cause: the invoice sync only fetched Type=="ACCREC"
Invoices and skipped everything else. Credit notes are a SEPARATE Xero document type
(ACCRECCREDIT) on the /CreditNotes endpoint, so they were never pulled — a customer
credit note didn't reduce the invoiced/sales figure (and therefore didn't reduce
retention or the EOM invoiced column).

THE FIX
-------
sync-invoices now ALSO fetches sales credit notes (ACCRECCREDIT) for the same window
and merges them into the invoice list with their amounts NEGATED (sales200,
retention612, total, subTotal, tax all negative). They're matched to projects by the
same tracking-name method, so:
  • Project Financials "Invoiced" nets off credit notes.
  • Retention (retention % × invoiced sales200) reduces accordingly.
  • The EOM report invoiced column reflects them.
Downstream sums use reduce() with no positive-only filter, so negatives net correctly.

FILES
-----
• pages/api/sync-invoices.js

AFTER DEPLOY
------------
Run "Sync Invoices" (Project Financials) to re-pull the window including credit notes.
The dashboard cache refreshes from the new figures.

STILL TO CONFIRM (cost side)
----------------------------
SUPPLIER credit notes (ACCPAYCREDIT) reduce COSTS. Bills are imported via the CSV
bulk upload, not this API sync — so supplier credits are only captured if they appear
in your bills CSV export (with negative amounts). If supplier credit notes are also
being missed on the cost side, tell me and I'll add an equivalent — but that depends
on how your bills are brought in (CSV vs API).
