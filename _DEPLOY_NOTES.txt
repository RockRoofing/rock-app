DEPLOY NOTES - THE FIX: Xero was missing the Journals permission (401)
======================================================================

ROOT CAUSE (confirmed by your diag)
-----------------------------------
The diag showed:
  fetch: {"pages":0, ... "lastError":"HTTP 401: AuthorizationUnsuccessful"}
The Xero Journals (general ledger) endpoint returned 401 on the FIRST request - while
your P&L, invoices and bills calls all work with the same token. That happens when the
connection is missing a specific PERMISSION (OAuth scope).

Your Xero connection was requested with these scopes (pages/connect.js):
  openid offline_access accounting.invoices.read accounting.contacts.read
  accounting.reports.profitandloss.read accounting.settings.read
  accounting.manualjournals.read projects.read
It was NOT asking for accounting.journals.read - which is the exact permission the
general-ledger (Journals) endpoint requires. So the sales table (and the overhead
transaction drill-down) had no data, even though the codes were correct.

THE FIX
-------
Added accounting.journals.read to the Xero connect scopes.

  *** THIS REQUIRES RECONNECTING XERO ***
  The permission is granted at connect time. Your current token was issued WITHOUT it,
  so you must reconnect to get a new token that includes it.

STEPS (important - in order)
----------------------------
1. Deploy pages/connect.js. Commit -> Vercel "Ready".
2. Go to the Xero connect page: https://app.rockroofing.co.uk/connect
   (or the "Connect Xero" link in the Admin centre).
3. Click connect and COMPLETE THE XERO CONSENT SCREEN. You should now see Xero ask for
   an additional permission about "journals" / "transactions" - approve it.
4. Back in the app, open Sales -> click "Sync Xero figures" and let it finish.
5. The detail table should now populate (and the Budgets cell drill-down too).

HOW TO CONFIRM IT WORKED
------------------------
If the table is still empty, check the diag line again - the "fetch" part should now
show pages > 0 and lastError: null. If it STILL says 401, the reconsent didn't take -
tell me and we'll check the Xero app config.

NOTE ON THE XERO DEVELOPER APP
------------------------------
Occasionally the scope also has to be enabled on the Xero app itself (developer.xero.com
-> your app -> configuration). If reconnecting still 401s, that's the next place to look;
let me know and I'll guide you.
