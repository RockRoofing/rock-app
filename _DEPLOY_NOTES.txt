CHANGE
------
In the "last month's manual adjustments" info area, the green carry-forward tick can
now be UN-ticked:
  • Tick (grey -> green): adds the adjustment to the current month for that project.
  • Tick again (green -> grey): removes that carried adjustment from the current month
    again (deletes the this-month copy).
The item colour and total update accordingly each time.
