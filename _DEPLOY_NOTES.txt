GANTT — copy & paste labour (in the yellow selection bar)

pages/operations/project-management/planning.js

HOW IT WORKS
  1. Select the cells you want to copy (a gang across days on a project). The yellow selection bar now has
     a blue "Copy" button (shows when the selection has labour).
  2. Click Copy — it captures the labour (named installers, unnamed headcount and status) for those days,
     keyed by day-offset from the first selected day.
  3. Select the START cell somewhere else — another project and/or another week (single cell is enough; the
     paste re-creates the full pattern from that day).
  4. A green "Paste (N days)" button appears in the bar. Click it to write the copied gang starting at the
     first selected day, preserving the day pattern (e.g. a Mon–Fri gang pasted from a Monday lands Mon–Fri).

DETAILS
  - Overwrite: if target days already have labour, you're warned before it overwrites them.
  - Clashes: if a copied installer is already booked on ANOTHER project that day, they're skipped for that
    day (the rest still pastes) and you get a summary of who was skipped.
  - "Actual" status isn't valid on today/future, so pasted "actual" days become "Confirmed" when they land
    on today or later.
  - The clipboard stays until you paste again or hit "Clear clipboard", so you can paste to several places.
