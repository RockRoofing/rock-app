DEPLOY NOTES - Overhead Budgets: bar chart, reconciliation, 3 cards
===================================================================

NEW FEATURES
------------
1) BAR CHART (top of page) - actual vs forecast per month, FY:
     - Blue bar  = Actual (completed months)
     - Grey bar  = Forecast for that same completed month (sits beside the blue)
     - Light-blue bar = Forecast for future months
   Respects the gear (only visible accounts are charted).

2) RECONCILIATION CHECKBOX - "Reconcile: all accounts vs shown". When ticked it shows
   the projected-year total across ALL overhead accounts vs the currently-SHOWN
   (unhidden) accounts. The box is GREEN when they match and RED when they differ
   (with the difference and a nudge to unhide accounts via the gear). Use it to make
   sure hiding rows hasn't dropped a category you needed.

3) THREE CARDS at the top:
   - Locked-in forecast (FYxxxx): shows the last locked full-year forecast and its
     date. "Lock in current" button snapshots this year's projected total (actual to
     date + forecast). "History" reveals previous locked figures with the dates they
     were locked. (Stored server-side in config:overhead-forecast-locks, last 24 kept.)
   - Last 3 months avg overheads: average monthly overhead over the last 3 completed
     months (shows which months + the total), visible accounts.
   - Forecast overheads (FYxxxx): actual to date + forecast for remaining months
     (= the projected year).
