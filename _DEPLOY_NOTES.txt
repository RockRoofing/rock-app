FIX A — Retention "Invoiced Net" wasn't updating on Sync Invoices
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Cause: sync-invoices stored totalInvoiced (VAT-inclusive) but NOT the ex-VAT
(net) total, VAT total, or VAT label. The Retention page's "Invoiced Net" reads
invoicedExVat, which the sync left stale/zero.
Fix (pages/api/sync-invoices.js): the sync now also computes and stores:
  • invoicedExVat = Σ line SubTotal  (net of VAT)
  • vatTotal      = Σ line TotalTax
  • vatRateLabel  (derived, e.g. "20%", "0%")
So Sync Invoices now updates Invoiced Net correctly.

Confirmed definition (as you specified): Invoiced Net = net of VAT (SubTotal),
NOT net of retention. That was already the intended reading; it just wasn't being
fed by the sync.

FIX B — Budget Tracker "Gross Invoiced" now NET of VAT, including retention
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Was: totalInvoiced (VAT-INCLUSIVE) + retentionOutstanding.
Now: invoicedExVat (NET of VAT) + retentionOutstanding.
For consistency (AFA and retention are net concepts), the dashboard now bases
these all on the net figure:
  • totalRetention   = invoicedExVat × pct/(1−pct)   (was on VAT-inclusive)
  • grossInvoiced    = invoicedExVat + retentionOutstanding
  • remainingToClaim = afa − invoicedExVat            (afa is net; was mixing net vs gross)
  • "fully invoiced/closed" check now compares invoicedExVat vs afa
totalInvoiced (VAT-inclusive) is still returned for anything that needs the gross.

Knock-on fixed: Retention page seeded release values as (grossInvoiced −
totalInvoiced)/2, which broke once grossInvoiced became net-based. Dashboard now
returns totalRetention; Retention uses totalRetention/2 per release.
