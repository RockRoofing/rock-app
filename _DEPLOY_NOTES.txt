WATER INGRESS — past visits now show as Missing (gantt is the source of truth)

pages/api/forms-missing.js
  FIX: a water-ingress WIRF is now REQUIRED once the visit has happened — i.e. it's marked Actual OR the
  visit date has PASSED. Previously only "Actual" counted, so a past visit that wasn't ticked actual
  silently dropped off the missing list. Now:
    • Past date (today has passed), not confirmed actual  -> MISSING (required)
    • Marked Actual                                        -> required
    • Future within 2 weeks, not actual                    -> "Upcoming" (visibility only)
    • Future beyond 2 weeks                                -> not shown yet

  SOURCE OF TRUTH = THE GANTT: all WIRF requirements are derived live from the gantt water-ingress data
  (ops:water-ingress) on every request — nothing is stored separately. So if a visit is MOVED on the gantt
  the required form moves with it, and if it's DELETED the form requirement disappears automatically.

  Verified: yesterday(not actual)=Missing, actual=required, +5d=Upcoming, +30d=hidden, today=Upcoming.

Deploy this file. No cron/env change.
