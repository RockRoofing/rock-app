Commercial Scorecard — Gross Margin now matches the EOM report
==============================================================
Deploy target: RockRoofing/rock-app  branch main
Build: Compiled successfully

WHY THEY DIDN'T MATCH
---------------------
The Scorecard's Gross Profit Margin used each project's ALL-TIME grossInvoiced and
totalCosts. The EOM report uses figures UP TO the selected month's valuation date.
Different periods -> different margin.

THE FIX
-------
The Scorecard now computes Gross Profit Margin on the EOM basis for the LAST FULL
MONTH, over in-progress projects only:
  • For each in-progress project, it finds the valuation date for the last completed
    month (per-month override, else the fixed valuation day).
  • Sums costs and account-200 invoiced UP TO that valuation date (reading the same
    costs:lines / invoiced:lines as EOM).
  • Gross Margin = (Σ invoiced − Σ costs) / Σ invoiced   (pooled, same as EOM totals).
Projects with no valuation date for that month are excluded (they aren't in the EOM
snapshot either).

The card sub-text now shows the month it reflects, e.g. "at June 2025 valuation (EOM
basis)".

FILES

• pages/commercial-scorecard.js     (card sub-text shows the month)
