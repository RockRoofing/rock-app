WHAT THIS IS
------------
Part A of the 13-week cash flow. This is the editor where you set WHEN each overhead's
monthly budget lands, so Part B (the forecast) can time it into weeks. Nothing is
scheduled by default - you configure each line you want in the forecast.

New tab: Summary | Sales | Budgets | Bills | Invoices Owed | Retentions Due | VAT Refund |
Cash Schedule | Cash Flow.

PER OVERHEAD LINE you choose a Timing:
  - (not in forecast)  - default; the line is ignored by cash flow.
  - Spread evenly      - the monthly budget is divided across the month's weeks.
  - One day            - whole monthly amount lands on a day you pick (e.g. PAYE 22nd).
  - Specific days      - split the month across several dates; must add up to the budget
                         (it shows "Balances to budget" / "£X unallocated").
Plus CARRY FORWARD on any scheduled line: move £X from one month to a later month without
touching the budgets (e.g. defer a payment).

The monthly AMOUNTS still come from the Budgets page (per code). This page only sets the
TIMING. There's a link through to Budgets at the top.

Saves to Redis (config:overhead-cashflow-schedule). A sticky "Save schedule" bar appears
when you have unsaved changes.
