PROJECT REPORT — Customer Company
  pages/operations/project-management/report.js
   - Renamed "Customer Name" to "Customer Company" (form field, table header, filter, view label).
   - The field auto-fills from the project's IHM customer company (settings.customerCompany, exposed
     by /api/ops-projects as `customer`) when a project is selected. It stays editable.

IMPORTANT: auto-fill only works if the project's IHM has the Customer Company filled in. If it's blank
on the IHM, the field will be empty (type it manually, or fill it on the IHM). This was the reason it
"wasn't inserting" — the wiring is correct; the source field was empty.
