ABOUT THE RECONCILE CHECKBOX (you asked)
----------------------------------------
It's a safety check for when you hide accounts with the main gear. Ticked, it compares
the projected-year total of ALL overhead accounts vs just the SHOWN ones: GREEN if they
match (what you see is everything), RED if they differ (you've hidden something, with
the gap shown). If you don't find it useful, say and I'll remove it.
