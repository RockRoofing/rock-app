ALL FROM THE IHM (same one-way principle as before)
---------------------------------------------------
Site address, order reference, customer company, customer address and retention %
are pulled from the Internal Handover Minutes (matched by job number):
      override (Project Details)  →  IHM value  →  blank
Editing any of them sets an override that STICKS and WINS, and NEVER writes back
to the IHM (one-way only). "from IHM" / "override" badges show the current source.
Retention in the IHM is free text (e.g. "5%"); it's parsed to a percentage.

IMPORTANT — RETENTION & FINANCIAL CALCS
---------------------------------------
Retention % drives WIP/retention maths (which read settings.retentionPct). On
SAVE, the effective retention (override → IHM → existing) is written into
settings.retentionPct, so the calcs stay correct. NOTE: for an IHM-sourced value
to flow into the financial calcs and the commercial pages, the project must be
SAVED once (the edit form shows the IHM value immediately; saving commits it).
