EOM invoiced now on the SAME basis as Budget Tracker (fixes the mismatch)
=========================================================================
Deploy target: RockRoofing/rock-app  branch main
Build: ✓ Compiled successfully

WHY THEY DIFFERED
-----------------
Budget Tracker "Invoiced" = sum of account-code-200 (Sales) lines: NET of VAT,
INCLUDING retention. EOM was still computing invoiced the OLD way: summing each
invoice's gross Total (VAT-INCLUSIVE) and then ADDING reconstructed retention on
top. So EOM read higher (VAT included + retention double-counted), on a different
basis entirely.

FIX (pages/commercial.js — calcAtValDate)
-----------------------------------------
EOM now sums the 200-coded Sales lines up to the valuation date (net of VAT, incl
retention), and does NOT re-add retention. This matches the Budget Tracker basis.
Retention is derived for display only.

After this, the ONLY expected difference between the two is the intended DATE
CUTOFF: Budget Tracker = all invoices to date; EOM = invoices up to the selected
month's valuation date. For the latest month they should agree.
