THE BUG (your June screenshot)
------------------------------
"Categorised in app" was LARGER than "Total in Xero" (giving negative "still to
find" on Bills −£28,865 and Sales −£147,803). Cause: the app figure summed the
whole tab dataset (data.bills / data.wages / data.invoices), which contains BOTH
categorised AND uncategorised items — so it double-counted the untagged ones into
the "categorised" figure.

FIX
---
"Categorised in app" now sums ONLY items where categorised === true. So:
  • Total in Xero        = the P&L figure
  • Categorised in app   = only what's actually allocated to a project
  • Still to find        = Xero − categorised (the amount left to allocate)
This makes "still to find" the true outstanding amount, and it should now be
positive (or zero when fully allocated), matching the uncategorised total.

REMAINING HONEST CAVEAT (Sales)
-------------------------------
Sales may still not tie to the penny because the app uses INVOICE DATE while the
Xero P&L income is TRANSACTION-DATE based (as previously agreed). Bills & Wages
should tie closely. If exact Sales reconciliation matters, we'd need a
transaction-date sales export.
