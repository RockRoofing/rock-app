Contracted Rates — bulk move across the line from the sticky bar
===============================================================
Deploy target: RockRoofing/rock-app  branch main
Build: ✓ Compiled successfully  ·  tab shows "· v13"

CHANGE
------
With items selected, the sticky bar now shows bulk move buttons:
  • When BELOW-the-line items are selected: "↑ Move N above the line".
  • When ABOVE-the-line items are selected: "↓ Move N below the line".
Moved items are appended (in their current order) after the last item of the
target section, and can then be reordered/dragged as usual. Works alongside the
existing bulk B/U/A formatting and Combine-into-variation buttons.
