DELIVERIES — enhancements

  pages/operations/project-management/deliveries.js  (portal)
   - When PO Sent, Supplier Confirmed, or 2nd Check is switched to "Yes", a prompt asks
     "Is the required delivery date of <date> still applicable?" reminding you to update it in the
     Required Delivery column if not.

  pages/forms/deliveries.js  (Site App)
   - Required Delivery Date now shown on each delivery card (and already in the editor).
   - PO line items are listed on SEPARATE bulleted lines (card + editor).
   - Proof warning reworded to: "Please attach a delivery note or photos. If not available, tap Save anyway."
   - The list now only shows deliveries that are EITHER not yet marked delivered OR due within the next
     2 weeks (delivered items outside that window are hidden to keep the site view focused).

  pages/api/cron/deliveries-notify.js  (NEW)  — runDeliveriesNotify()
   - Finds deliveries whose Required Delivery Date is TODAY and not yet delivered, grouped by project.
   - Emails the effective site supervisor (Project Details supervisor if on the Gantt, else the first
     qualified Gantt supervisor) a list of expected deliveries (PO, supplier, bulleted items), a note to
     call the Rock Roofing Operations Manager (name + phone + email pulled from Team) for more info, and a
     reminder to mark each delivery as delivered in the Site App and attach the delivery note + photos.

  pages/api/cron/hs-expiry-email.js  (daily dispatcher)
   - Now also runs the deliveries notification EVERY day at 07:00 (alongside the Monday forms digest and
     1st-of-month H&S expiry). No new Vercel cron is added — stays within the Hobby 2-cron limit.
