CHANGE
------
In the Upcoming Applications table, the "Set up" button (shown for projects that
need contracted rates) now links straight to:
    /contracted-rates?projectId=<xeroId>
The Contracted Rates page reads that query parameter on load and auto-selects the
project, so you land ready to upload the costing document immediately — no need to
re-pick the project.
