FIRST, THE "SALES INVOICES MIXED IN" POINT
-------------------------------------------
The Bills page data already comes only from Xero SUPPLIER bills (Type=ACCPAY,
AUTHORISED, AmountDue>0) - sales invoices (ACCREC) are a separate feed used by the
Outstanding Invoices page. So the backend was already bills-only. If you saw sales
invoices, it was likely stale/looking at the wrong page. This rebuild makes it explicit
(title now says "supplier bills only", footer notes ACCPAY) and everything on the page
is bills. If you STILL see anything that looks like a customer/sales invoice after a
"Sync bills", tell me the supplier name shown and I'll trace that specific record.
