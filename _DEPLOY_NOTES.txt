THE DARREN BUG — FIXED
----------------------
Root cause: bill dedupe key was reference|accountCode|amount, which COLLAPSED
genuinely-distinct lines (multi-project invoices, and fortnightly repeats on one
invoice number). Darren had 4 real June lines (£650, £1500, £800, £800 = £3,750)
but only £650 showed.

NEW BEHAVIOUR — REPLACE BY DATE RANGE (Bills)
---------------------------------------------
Bills upload now makes the app an EXACT MIRROR of the file for the period it
covers:
  • Detects the file's date range (min..max InvoiceDate).
  • For that range, across ALL projects, it REPLACES existing bills with the
    file's lines verbatim (no dedupe guessing — genuine repeats kept).
  • Lines OUTSIDE the range are untouched (older uploads preserved).
  • Projects NOT in the file lose their in-range bills (exact mirror: if it's not
    in Xero's export for that range, it's not in the app).
  • Untagged/overhead bills handled the same way (replace-by-range).
Runtime-tested: Darren J243 June now stores all 4 lines = £3,750.
Response reports: rangeFrom/rangeTo, linesReplacedInRange, projectsClearedInRange.

NO WARNING/CONFIRM: per your instruction — if it's not in Xero it shouldn't be in
the app, so the replace just happens and the result reports what changed.

GLOBAL NIGHTLY SALES + WAGES (all projects, 6-month window)
-----------------------------------------------------------
Previously the nightly sync refreshed sales/wages for only ONE project per night
(rotation), so a project's newly-categorised sales could take ~weeks to appear.
Now, once per day, the sync refreshes SALES and WAGES for EVERY project over a
6-month window (exact-mirror within the window; older data preserved). Bills stay
on the per-project nightly rotation (per-bill detail is the expensive call).
API cost: sales/wages are batched endpoints (~6-15 calls each across all
projects) — well within limits. Guarded once/day via 'global-sync:at'.
Response reports globalSalesProjects / globalWagesProjects.

NOTE: This fixes "categorised sales in Xero not showing" within a day
automatically. The on-demand Sync buttons come in part 2/2.
