WHAT'S NEW
----------
• When a chase email is SENT, it's automatically logged as a comment on that
  invoice, e.g.:  "📧 Overdue 1 email sent to bob@acme.co.uk — "Overdue invoice
  INV-1234"".  Because comments are timestamped, the sent emails now appear
  INTERLEAVED CHRONOLOGICALLY with manual comments in the invoice's comment
  pop-out (a small "chase sent" badge marks them).
• These comments are stored in the invoice meta, so they AUTOMATICALLY flow into
  the WEEKLY REPORT (which already lists each invoice's comments/emails to date).
  No separate change needed there.
• Manual timeline ticks (Phase 3) do NOT create a comment — only actual sends do
  (a manual tick means "chased another way", so no email log line).

Only SENT chase emails are logged. Customer replies are NOT captured (by your
decision) — that would need an inbound email address + webhook, which we can add
later if wanted.
