trend value as the raw fraction (0.193) and letting pct() do the single x100. The
plotted line looks identical (chart auto-scales); only the tooltip is corrected.

format differently from the headline (headline stays a whole number for a month's
count; the average shows 1 dp). Verified: 0.6667 -> "0.7".
