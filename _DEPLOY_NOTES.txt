DEPLOY NOTES - Retentions Invoiced: % ticked over last 6 months
================================================================

WHAT CHANGED
------------
The "Retentions Invoiced" card on the Commercial Scorecard now:

  - Shows the last 6 COMPLETED months as clickable chips (was 12 months).
  - Each chip toggles Yes / No on click (unchanged behaviour).
  - Months NOT ticked stay visible - shown red with "No" (they don't disappear).
  - Headline is now a PERCENTAGE: how often retention was ticked across those
    6 months (e.g. 4 of 6 = 66.7%), instead of a bare Yes/No for one month.
  - A small line under the chips reads "Ticked X of 6 months (last 6)".
  - Target defaults to 100% and is editable like the other cards (enter as a
    fraction, e.g. 1 = 100%, matching how Gross Margin's target works).

The tick data itself is unchanged and still stored the same way
(commercial:retention_invoiced, keyed 'YYYY-MM' -> true/false), so all existing
ticks carry over. No API change.

VERIFY
------
  - Open the Commercial Scorecard, find "Retentions Invoiced".
  - You should see 6 month chips (the last 6 completed months). Click any to
    toggle Yes/No - unticked ones stay red, they don't vanish.
  - The big number shows the % of those 6 months that are ticked, and the RAG
    dot goes green at/above target (100% by default), amber near it, red below.
