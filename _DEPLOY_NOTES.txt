WHY YOU SAW NO BREAKDOWN
------------------------
The GP Margin card opened the generic drill modal, which returned nothing when the
breakdown list was empty (or if the click didn't register as a drill). So clicking
appeared to do nothing.

FIX
---
• Clicking the Gross Profit Margin card now ALWAYS opens the breakdown modal.
• If the breakdown is empty, it shows a message instead of silently doing nothing.
• The breakdown lists, per in-progress project for the last full month:
  Project, Project Name, Valuation Date (or "— (excluded)"), Invoiced, Costs, Margin.

HOW TO SEND ME THE NUMBERS
--------------------------
1. Open the Commercial Scorecard, click the Gross Profit Margin card -> the breakdown
   opens.
2. Screenshot it (or copy the rows).
3. Open the EOM report (Project Financials, EOM mode) for the SAME month, in-progress,
   and screenshot the equivalent columns.
4. Send both. Rows where Invoiced/Costs differ, or projects shown "excluded" here but
   present in EOM, will pinpoint the cause.
