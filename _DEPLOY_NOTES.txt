PAGES
-----
• SUMMARY (existing) — sales, gross margin, wages vs subcontract, cost-of-sale pie,
  cash in/out. Nav now links to all the pages below.
• BUDGETS — set a monthly budget per category (Sales / Cost of sales / Overheads).
  Table shows each month's actual vs budget, green when on/under (costs) or on/above
  (sales), red when off track. Saved to config:business-budgets.
• BILLS TO PAY — total outstanding at top; selectable line-item table (running total
  of selected); bar chart of bills due per month (past + upcoming, "now" marker).
  "Sync bills" pulls outstanding ACCPAY bills from Xero.
• INVOICES OWED — date-range filter (defaults to 12 months, 2 into the future); bar
  chart of invoices falling due per month; detail table. "Sync invoices" pulls
  outstanding ACCREC invoices from Xero.
• CASH FLOW — 8–10 week forecast: known invoices in vs known bills + predicted
  overheads (recent 3-mo overhead average, spread weekly). Cash-at-bank line shows
  history (actual closing balances) then the projected weekly balance. Start cash
  comes from Xero (latest bank closing balance) and can be overridden to model
  scenarios.

NEW XERO FETCHES (lib/xero.js)
------------------------------
• fetchBankSummary        — cash in/out + opening/closing balance per period.
• fetchOutstandingBills   — authorised ACCPAY with amount due > 0.
• fetchOutstandingReceivables — authorised ACCREC with amount due > 0.

DATA / REDIS KEYS
-----------------
• xero:pl-benchmark          (existing) — Summary charts 1–4, Budgets actuals.
• bank:summary-by-month      — cash in/out + balances (Summary "Sync bank").
• bank:outstanding-bills     — Bills to Pay ("Sync bills").
• bank:outstanding-receivables — Invoices Owed ("Sync invoices").
• config:business-budgets    — saved budgets.

AFTER DEPLOY (to populate everything)
-------------------------------------
1. Bookkeeping → "Sync Xero figures"  (P&L benchmark).
2. Business Financials → Summary → "Sync bank".
3. Bills to Pay → "Sync bills".
4. Invoices Owed → "Sync invoices".
5. Set budgets on the Budgets page.
Cash Flow then assembles automatically from the above.
