NEW FEATURES
------------
1) MONTHLY TARGET: editable figure in the filter bar (top). Saved server-side
   (config:sales-monthly-target). Drawn as a grey dashed line on the chart.
2) AVERAGE LINE: the average revenue across the shown period, as a dashed line -
   GREEN if the average is at/above target, RED if below.
3) KEY for the three dashed lines (Target / Average / Now) above the chart.
4) FINANCIAL-YEAR FILTER: a FY selector (Dec-Nov) that sets the date range to that year
   (last FY, this FY, etc.). You can then still fine-tune with the From/To date inputs.
   "Reset to FY" snaps the range back to the selected financial year.
5) CLICK A BAR: clicking a month's bar filters the table to just that month's
   transactions (and highlights the bar). Click again, or "clear", to show all.
