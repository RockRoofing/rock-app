WHAT THE DIAG PROVED
--------------------
Your diag: chart codes {200, 260, 270}, requested {200,260,270}, but LEDGER CODES [] /
0 lines. So the codes are RIGHT - the general-ledger (Xero Journals) fetch itself is

WHY (most likely)
-----------------
The Journals endpoint returns journals oldest-first, 100 at a time, paged by offset.
For a company with a long history there are thousands of journals; the code has to page
through ALL of them to reach the last 13 months. Two possible failures:
  (a) the request errors (e.g. a permissions/scope issue on Journals) and the old code
      silently returned empty; or
  (b) it runs out of time/pages before reaching recent journals.
