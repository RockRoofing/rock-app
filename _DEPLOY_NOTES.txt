WHY IT DIDN'T MATCH (confirmed from your screenshots)
-----------------------------------------------------
Xero's "Awaiting payment" nets SUPPLIER CREDIT NOTES against bills. Examples from your
Xero screen: UK Postbox (24.65), Fixfast "CN re INV 1558824" (136.08) - negative
balances that REDUCE the total. The app only pulled ACCPAY invoices and excluded
anything <= 0, so those credits were missing and the app total sat higher than Xero.
(Also, Xero's screen was filtered by transaction date; the app filters by due date -
different field, slightly different set.)

WHAT CHANGED
------------
1) The bills fetch now ALSO pulls outstanding ACCPAY credit notes (Xero CreditNotes,

   lines. The net total now matches Xero's "Awaiting payment".
2) Credit-note rows are shown with a green "CREDIT" tag and a negative (green) amount,
   on a light-green row, so they're obvious and clearly not something to "pay".
3) Totals (All outstanding / Filtered / Selected) all net the credits automatically.
