WILL & MIKE (Contracts Managers — identical metric set, data by their name):
  1. Gross margin — their projects. Uses the Commercial/Project Financials CM designation; only LIVE
     (INPROGRESS) and DEFECTS-period projects. Shows profit + project count.
  2. H&S incidences — projects with an Accident Book OR Accident & Incident Report submitted in the month;
     if both exist for the same project in the month it counts ONCE.
  3. Water Ingress — Rock at fault — count of Water Ingress reports (all time, their projects) where
     "Who is responsible" = Rock.
  4. Pre-Start Notifications submitted — PSNs they submitted in the month (unique to that CM).
  5. Procurement savings incomplete — their projects completed in the month whose procurement savings doc
     isn't fully complete.
  6. Issues resolved late — issues where the required resolution date is before the resolved date.

DORI (Operations Manager):

  3. Work Area Handovers — completed this month.
  4. Toolbox Talk — manual Yes/No per month (green/red toggle on the card; saved per month).
  5. Tasks resolved overdue — Live Project Tasks closed after their target date.
  6. Risk log items resolved late — resolved after the target resolution date.
