The examples you sent (LINDUM Construction INV-1533, Deeley Construction INV-1663, etc.)

certainly written by an OLDER sync before bills/sales were separated. No banner appeared

THE FIX
-------
The page now shows ONLY records explicitly stamped type:'ACCPAY' (supplier bills). Any
record without that stamp is treated as stale/mis-stored and hidden, with a banner
telling you to sync. Because the fetch (from the previous update) stamps every bill with
type:'ACCPAY', a single "Sync bills" fully REPLACES the stored data with correctly-typed
supplier bills and the sales invoices disappear for good.
