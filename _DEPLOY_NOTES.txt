WHY YOU SAW "0 BILLS IMPORTED" FOR 2023
---------------------------------------
"New lines added" only counts bills that matched a CURRENT project. For a 2023
file, most lines are either:
  • untagged (no Projects tracking category — common for older bills), or
  • tagged to projects that are now ARCHIVED (so they don't match a live project).
Either way, nothing lands against a current project -> "0". BUT the untagged lines
ARE still captured — into the Bookkeeping page (Costs (Bills) tab) as unassigned.
So "0 imported" was the message, not lost data.

WHAT CHANGED
------------
The Bills/Wages upload result now spells this out, so 0 is never ambiguous:
  • "N line(s) had no project tag (X new) — captured under Bookkeeping … as unassigned."
  • "N project tag(s) didn't match a current project (e.g. archived) and were skipped."
  • When new-added is 0 but there were untagged/unmatched lines, a note explains
    it doesn't mean nothing imported.

So for your 2023 bills: check the Bookkeeping page -> Costs (Bills) tab -> filter to
2023 months; the untagged older bills will be listed there.
