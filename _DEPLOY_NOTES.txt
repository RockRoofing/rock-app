WATER INGRESS — "missing form" (WIRF) logic per your rules

pages/api/forms-missing.js — reworked the Water Ingress Report requirement:
  • 1 WIRF per water-ingress VISIT EVENT for a job.
  • Consecutive days on the same job = ONE event — only the first day needs a WIRF (a continuation day
    adds nothing).
  • A gap of ≥1 day starts a NEW event, which needs its own WIRF.
  • 2 separate visits on the SAME day both need a WIRF.
  • SOLIDIFIES as "required" only once the visit is marked ACTUAL on the Gantt.
  • Visits within the NEXT 2 WEEKS that aren't yet actual show as "Upcoming" (visibility/notification) but
    do NOT count as required/missing until they're marked actual.
  • "done" = a Water Ingress Report form submitted for that job in the visit's week (unchanged matching).
  • Upcoming rows are excluded from the required/completed/% summary and the byForm tally.

pages/operations/forms-missing.js (portal Forms — Missing page):
  • Water-ingress visits due in the next 2 weeks show a blue "Upcoming" badge (tinted row) instead of red
    "Missing".
  • The "Missing" filter no longer includes Upcoming rows.

Verified with a test: Mon+Tue consecutive + Thu (2 visits) on one job = 3 WIRFs (Mon 1, Tue 0, Thu 2).
