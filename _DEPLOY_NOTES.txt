Financial App — Outstanding Invoices: Outstanding/All toggle + 50-per-page
==========================================================================
Deploy target: RockRoofing/rock-app  branch main

Replaces the outstanding-invoices page (deploy over the top).

WHAT CHANGED
------------
• PAID INVOICES no longer just vanish. A toggle at the top defaults to
  "Outstanding" (invoices with a balance due) and can switch to "All" (includes
  fully-paid invoices). Paid rows show a green "PAID" badge and a green-tinted
  row; their Due shows £0 in green.
• PAGINATION: max 50 invoices per page, with Prev / Next and a "Page X of Y"
  indicator. Page resets to 1 when you change the toggle, search or sort.
• Summary cards + the count line adapt to the current view.

NOTES (answers to your questions)
---------------------------------
• Invoice data comes from the CSV import on the RETENTION TRACKER
  ("⬆ Import invoices from Xero") — export Sales Invoices from Xero and upload it
  there. You do NOT upload invoices into Project Financials (that's costs/margins,
  a different Xero data set). One upload feeds both Retention Tracker and this page.
• You can re-upload as often as you like. Each import is a full snapshot that
  OVERWRITES the previous figures per project — so there are no duplicates and no
  double-counting. Always export the FULL invoice list (not a filtered subset).
• The downloadable / weekly report remains OUTSTANDING-only (it's a chase report),
  regardless of the on-screen toggle.
