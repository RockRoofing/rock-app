DEPLOY NOTES - Sales: fix May (wrong invoices pulled) + average = completed months only
=======================================================================================

WHY MAY WAS WRONG (found by comparing your app vs Xero line-by-line)
-------------------------------------------------------------------
The May LINE LIST in the app was a DIFFERENT set of invoices than Xero's May list
(app showed Bosden/Atlas/Farmstead/J190/J197...; Xero showed Vale Southern/MAYLIM/
Trident/Barnfield/Selsdon/Torsion...). That's because the fetch was sending an
"If-Modified-Since" header, which tells Xero "only return records MODIFIED since date X"
- a modification filter, NOT a date filter. So the app pulled a partial/incorrect set of
invoices, then bucketed them by date - giving the wrong invoices in the wrong months.
Most months happened to look right; May didn't.

THE FIX
-------
Removed "If-Modified-Since" from the invoice, credit-note and bank-transaction fetches.
They now pull the full set and keep everything dated on/after the window start, bucketed
by the Xero accounting date - matching Xero's period membership.

  *** REQUIRES A RE-SYNC ***  Deploy -> Sales -> "Sync Xero figures" to re-pull the
  correct invoice set. May (and any other month) should then match Xero.

AVERAGE = COMPLETED MONTHS ONLY
-------------------------------
The average dashed line now uses only COMPLETED months (excludes the current month,
which is always partial, and any future months). The key now reads:
  "Average of completed months (£x) - above/below target".
