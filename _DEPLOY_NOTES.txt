Commercial — add sync-period dropdown to the sync buttons
=========================================================
Deploy target: RockRoofing/rock-app  branch main
Build: ✓ Compiled successfully

WHY
---
The Squirrels showed only ~£29,925 invoiced because the sync window was 6 months
— its Dec-2025 and Jan-2026 invoices (£136k) fell outside it. Syncing 24 months
in Bookkeeping pulled them in and it corrected. So the Commercial sync buttons
needed the same selectable window.

CHANGE
------
• components/SyncBar.js — now has its OWN "Sync last [3/6/12/18/24 mo]" dropdown
  (like the Bookkeeping page), shown whenever Invoices or Wages buttons are shown.
  The chosen window is used for the sync. Default is now 12 months.
  (New prop showPeriod=true; pass showPeriod={false} to hide the dropdown.)
• commercial.js / retention.js / outstanding-invoices.js — default window set to
  12 months (was 6). Users can bump to 24 for long-running projects.

PERFORMANCE NOTE
----------------
Invoice sync pages the list 100 at a time and reads line items inline (no per-
invoice re-fetch), so even 24 months is a handful of calls — seconds, well within
Xero's 60/min limit. 12/24 months is safe.

REMINDER
--------
For a long-running project whose invoices predate the window, pick a wider window
(e.g. 24 mo) once and re-sync; older invoices are then stored and retained.
