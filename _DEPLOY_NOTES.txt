Contracted Rates — enhancements (totals, columns, selection)
============================================================
Deploy target: RockRoofing/rock-app  branch main
Build: ✓ Compiled successfully  ·  Contracted Rates tab shows "· v3"
Deploy on top of the Contracted Rates work (incl. the blank-row + upload/nav fixes).

1) "RATE ONLY" / "TBC" TEXT vs CALCULATED TOTAL
-----------------------------------------------
• On upload, any non-numeric text in the Total column (Rate only, TBC, P.O.A,
  etc.) is preserved verbatim.
• Each line's Total is now switchable. Edit a line → the Total cell shows a
  "Calculate / Text (TBC…)" selector:
    - Calculate  → Total = Qty × Rate (updates as you type a quantity).
    - Text       → type free text (e.g. "TBC", "Rate only").
  So a "Rate only" line becomes a real calculated total the moment you add a
  quantity and switch it to Calculate — or you can keep/enter text.

2) MATERIALS TOTAL + LABOUR TOTAL COLUMNS
-----------------------------------------
Two new BLUE budget columns: "Mat total" (Qty × Mat rate) sits right after Mat
rate, and "Lab total" (Qty × Lab rate) right after Lab rate.

3) LINE SELECTION + GROUP VALUE
-------------------------------
• Every line now has a tick-box on the left.
• Ticking lines shows a summary bar: selected count + Total rate + Materials +
  Labour for the selection.

4) SECTION TOTAL ROWS
---------------------
• A total row now sits at the BOTTOM of the above-the-line section (and one for
  below the line, above nothing — it's the last row of that block).
• ABOVE the line: always shows the full contract-works total (rate + materials +
  labour). When above-the-line lines are ticked, it switches to the ticked
  subtotal.
• BELOW the line: shows the full below-the-line total by default; when below-line
  lines are ticked, it shows the ticked subtotal.
• Text lines (TBC / Rate only) contribute 0 to the rate total (verified).

Site App read-only view updated to match (shows TBC/Rate-only text correctly).
