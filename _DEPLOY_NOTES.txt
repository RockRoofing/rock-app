CHANGE
------
In Contract Works, a line only shows a Total, % Complete box, ✓ tick and Value to
date when it is a COMPLETE MEASURABLE ITEM — i.e. it has a quantity, a unit, a rate
and a (non-zero) total. Lines missing any of those (sub-lines, notes, placeholders)
now leave those cells BLANK, and they don't contribute to the Contract Works total
or the Summary.

-----
• lib/applications.js     (isMeasurableWorks; totals + value-to-date gated on it)
• pages/applications.js   (blank cells for non-measurable rows; v9)
