WHAT THIS ADDS
--------------
A "⬇ Download Signed RAMS" button next to "+ Upload RAMS" on the project's RAMS
tab (Operations → Projects → RAMS).

Click it → a picker lists every RAMS REVISION (newest first, with its stage and
number of operative signatures). Choose one → it downloads a single PDF that is:
  • the ORIGINAL RAMS document, followed by
  • an appended "Signature & Approval Record" showing the full workflow:
      1. Contracts Manager — name, date/time signed, signature image
      2. Director — name, date/time signed, signature image
      3. Customer Site Manager — name, email, date/time approved (typed approval
         via the secure no-login link) OR, if they requested edits, the rejection
         with their comments and timestamp
      • Every OPERATIVE signature — name, date/time, and signature image, plus the
        confirmation statement they all agreed to
  • An overall status banner (e.g. STATUS: OPERATIVES, or EDITS REQUIRED) and a
    per-page footer with generation timestamp.

This is the electronic-footprint / confirmed-workflow version you were missing —
one download per revision, on demand (nothing to pre-generate or store).

DATA USED (all already captured by the workflow)
------------------------------------------------
• ops:rams-approvals — cm/director/siteManager (+ rejection) with signedAt times
• ops:rams-signatures — per-operative name, date, signedAt, statement, signature
CM/Director auto-signatures are shown in the approval chain (not double-listed
under operatives). The Site Manager approves by typing their name (no drawn
signature), so their entry is a typed confirmation with a timestamp.

TECHNICAL NOTES
---------------
• Uses pdf-lib (already in the project). The original PDF's pages are copied in,
  then audit pages appended. If the original can't be embedded (e.g. it's an
  image RAMS or an unreadable PDF), the record is still produced and notes that.
• Helvetica can't encode tick/cross glyphs, so status markers are drawn as
  coloured dots (green = done, red = not approved, outline = pending) — this was
  verified to avoid a PDF-generation crash.
