WHY THE TOTAL DIDN'T MATCH XERO
-------------------------------
It's a DATE-RANGE mismatch, not a data error:
  - Xero screen: "Awaiting payment", dates May 10 2024 -> Jul 31 2026 -> 108 bills,
    247,081.79.
  - App: was showing the FILTERED total (99 bills, 251,124) using the page's default
    due-date window of -12 to +12 months, which runs to Jul 2027 - a year FURTHER than
    your Xero screen. Different date windows = different sets of bills = different
    totals. The app pulls ALL outstanding AUTHORISED bills with AmountDue>0 (same
    definition as Xero's "Awaiting payment"); the page was just filtering them to a
    different window before totalling.

WHAT I CHANGED
--------------
Added an "All outstanding bills" figure (unfiltered) next to the Filtered and Selected
totals. THIS is the number that should reconcile to Xero's "Awaiting payment" total
when you set the same date range. The Filtered total still reflects whatever date/
supplier filters you've applied.
