WATER INGRESS — new planning section + WLA/email + forms

  pages/api/planning.js
   - New store ops:water-ingress = { [dateISO]: [ { id, jobName, jobAddress, projectNo, status, unnamed,

     old/unlisted job name; project ADDRESS is mandatory (auto or manual). Installers (CSCS/WAH hard-block
     applies), unnamed headcount, and a status (Confirmed/Provisional/Actual). Nothing auto-selected.

  pages/api/planning-week.js  (assembleWeek)
   - Water-ingress visits now flow into the Weekly Labour Allocation: each shows as "💧 <job name>" on the
     operative's row with its own address, so it appears in the WLA table, the WLA PDF, and the installer
     EMAIL (including under the email's "Project addresses" list). Future-only / non-Actual email rules
     still apply.

  pages/api/forms-missing.js + pages/operations/forms-missing.js
   - Standard forms are NOT required for water-ingress work. The only required form is a WATER INGRESS
     REPORT — one per job-visit (per job per day), shown in the Missing dashboard in the visit's week with
     its own card, %, and Missing/Completed status. Matched to a submitted form whose title contains
     "Water Ingress" for that job in that week.
