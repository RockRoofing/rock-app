WHAT WAS WRONG
--------------
Account Categorisation, Xero Upload and Data Management were removed from the
Admin nav, but the PAGES still rendered inside the Admin shell — so opening them
dropped you into the dark "Admin" bar ("← Portal | Admin"). Visually they were
still in the Admin section.

FIX
---
When AdminShell renders those three pages it now shows BOOKKEEPING chrome:
  • Top bar: "← Bookkeeping | Bookkeeping Tools" (was "← Portal | Admin")
  • Back link goes to /bookkeeping (not the portal/admin home)
  • Sub-nav: the three bookkeeping tools + "← All tools" (no Admin tabs)
The route stays /admin/... under the hood (no link-breaking file move), but
visually and navigationally these pages are now fully inside Bookkeeping — no
Admin chrome anywhere.
