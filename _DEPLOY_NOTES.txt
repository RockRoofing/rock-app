Single file: pages/retention.js (latest — includes all prior retention changes)

1) 1st / 2nd VALUE ONLY GO GREEN WHEN FA MATCHES INVOICED
   A release cell now turns green ("released") only when BOTH:
     • the amount paid means that release is settled, AND
     • the Final Account reconciles with the invoiced value (within £1).
   If it's paid/settled but FA ≠ Invoiced, the cell shows an amber "⚠ check FA"
   warning instead of green (tooltip explains why). Still-due releases stay orange.

2) FINAL ACCOUNT WARNING WHEN INVOICED IS HIGHER
   When the invoiced value exceeds the Final Account, the FA figure turns RED and
   shows "⚠ FA lower than invoiced" beneath it, flagging the FA is understated and
   needs updating.
