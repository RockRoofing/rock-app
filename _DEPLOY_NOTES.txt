WHY NO 3rd "BILLS" UPLOAD
-------------------------
The Account Transactions report already includes bills, journals, subcontractor
labour (321) and direct wages (320) when you tick those accounts — so a separate
Bills export would double-count. Keeping ONE cost report (Account Transactions)
is simpler; the on-page note tells you to include all cost accounts so nothing is
missed.
