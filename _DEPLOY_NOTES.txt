WHAT'S IN THIS PHASE
--------------------
The Business Financials tile on the portal home (Admin only) now opens the Summary
page with five charts:
  1. Sales — invoiced sales at end of each month (line).
  2. Gross margin — (sales − cost of sales) ÷ sales, by month (line).
  3. Direct wages vs sub-contract labour — monthly split (bar). Direct = code 320;
     sub-contract = 321 + 328 + 334.
  4. Cost of sale by category — Labour / Materials / Overheads (pie), across the
     period, using the Account Categorisation.
  5. Cash in / cash out — ACTUAL money in and out each month (bar), from Xero's Bank
     Summary report.
A month-range selector (6/12/18/24) and a "Sync bank" button are at the top.

DATA SOURCES
------------
• Charts 1–4 read the existing P&L benchmark (xero:pl-benchmark). Keep it current via
  Bookkeeping → "Sync Xero figures".
• Chart 5 uses a NEW Xero Bank Summary fetch (fetchBankSummary in lib/xero.js),
  stored at bank:summary-by-month. Click "Sync bank" on the Summary page to populate/
  refresh it (pulls the last 18 months).

The nav shows the future tabs (Budgets, Bills to Pay, Invoices Owed, Cash Flow) as
greyed "coming soon" — those are the next phases.
