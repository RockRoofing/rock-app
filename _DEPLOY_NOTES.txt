HOW IT NOW WORKS - to answer your questions directly
----------------------------------------------------
- "Do I just add Box 5 and that's the VAT return?"  YES. For a past month, type the Box 5
  from your Xero VAT return and choose refund / to-pay. That IS the figure the page (and
  cash flow) uses for that month. You don't need the estimate to match it - the estimate
  is only a rough transaction guess and never includes late claims / reverse charge, so it
  won't equal the filed figure. That's expected.

- "Why does it show Position used for past dates - remove it?"  Kept, but cleaned up:
  * Past month WITH a filed Box 5 entered -> Source "Filed return", Position used = your
    filed figure. The estimate columns are hidden for past months now (they were just
    noise).
  * Past month WITHOUT a filed Box 5 yet -> Source "Estimate (enter filed)", and it falls
    back to the estimate as a placeholder so the forecast isn't blank (per your choice).
  * Current / future months -> estimate only (no return exists yet).

So past months now show ONLY the filed Box 5 (no estimate clutter beside it), which
removes the "looks inaccurate" side-by-side mismatch.
