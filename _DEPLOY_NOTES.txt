TICK BOXES + SELECTION-DRIVEN STAT CARDS
----------------------------------------
• A checkbox column has been added on the LEFT of every invoice row.
• Tick any rows and the top cards recompute for JUST those invoices:
    - "Selected invoices" (count) and "Selected total due" replace the normal
      Outstanding/Total labels, and Overdue count/value reflect the selection too.
    - A blue banner appears: "Showing totals for N selected invoices" with a
      "Clear selection" button, and the cards get a subtle blue border.
• With nothing ticked, the cards show all rows exactly as before.
• The header checkbox is a SELECT-ALL: it ticks/unticks every invoice in the
  current filtered set (all pages, not just the visible page), and shows an
  indeterminate (–) state when only some are selected.
• Selected rows are highlighted. Selection is by invoice number, so it survives
  paging/sorting.
