2) RETENTIONS DUE  (pages/business-financials/retentions-due.js)
---------------------------------------------------------------
Shows WHEN outstanding retention is due to land and its value. It loads the SAME sources
as the Retention Tracker (dashboard projects + manual retention entries + hidden list)
and merges them identically, then lists every release that is NOT yet received, by its
release date. Chart = due by month (click a bar to filter); table = the schedule.

BALANCES WITH THE TRACKER: the page sums each entry's outstanding balance
((release1+release2) minus received) exactly as the Tracker does. A green banner confirms
"Balanced with the Retention Tracker: outstanding X = total shown". If they ever differ
(e.g. a project marked Complete with a release still flagged unreceived) it shows an amber
banner explaining what to fix in the Tracker. Marking a release received in the Tracker
removes it here and reduces the total - so it moves in step with the Tracker.
