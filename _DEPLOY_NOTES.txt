WARNINGS NOW ALL SHOW AS RED LINES UNDER THE PROJECT NAME (no more small ⚠/⚑ symbols in front of the name):

1) "⚠ Man day allocation needed"
   - Shows when the project has NO man days / labour allocated on the Gantt at all.
   - (Renamed from "Project Programme dates needed!" and re-based on labour, not the date boxes — so
     Accrington, which HAS labour, no longer shows it.)

2) "⚠ Runs past completion date"
   - Shows when man days are allocated AFTER the contracted completion date.
   - PLUS: when this happens, the completion-date field goes RED + BOLD, and the project number/name go
     RED + BOLD.

3) "⚑ Historic dates need confirming actual"  (orange)
   - Now only flags when it's THURSDAY ONWARDS and there are unconfirmed (not-Actual) allocations in the
     CURRENT week…
   - …EXCEPTION: if the PREVIOUS week or the week before that still has unconfirmed allocations, it flags
     regardless of the day.

4) "⚠ No supervisor" — unchanged (already a red line).
