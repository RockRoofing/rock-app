Xero has them as 12 project-tagged journal lines (ref #96453). Syncing didn't fix
it. The wages sync had the SAME bugs the invoice sync had before its fix:

THE FIX (mirrors the invoice fix)

  "J242-Winnersh") — the reliable text match, same as CSV import.
• Group tagged lines per project; untagged -> unassigned wages bucket.
• Replace in-window wages for ALL projects + the untagged bucket (keep older than
  window). This REPLACES the old uploaded lump with the correctly-tagged journal
  lines.
