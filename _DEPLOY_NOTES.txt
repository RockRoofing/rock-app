TO ACTIVATE THE NEW FORMS after deploy (one-time, additive — does NOT wipe existing forms):
   POST https://app.rockroofing.co.uk/api/forms   body: {"action":"seed-missing"}
   (e.g. from the browser console:
      fetch('/api/forms',{method:'POST',headers:{'Content-Type':'application/json'},body:JSON.stringify({action:'seed-missing'})}).then(r=>r.json()).then(console.log)
   It returns { added:[...ids] }. )

Deploy all six files. No cron/env change.
